Movement

Common Moves

Stand from prone Costs half your speed.
Crawl Costs 1 extra foot per foot moved.
Climb / swim Costs 1 extra foot per foot moved unless you have that speed.

Terrain

Difficult terrain costs 1 extra foot of movement for each foot moved through it.

Jumping

  • Long jump: with a 10-foot run, jump up to your Strength score in feet.
  • Standing long jump: half that distance.
  • High jump: with a 10-foot run, jump 3 + Strength modifier feet.
  • Standing high jump: half that distance.

Squeezing

A creature can squeeze through a space one size smaller. Movement costs 1 extra foot per foot, attack rolls and Dexterity saves have disadvantage, and attacks against it have advantage.
