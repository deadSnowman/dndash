Resting

Short Rest

At least 1 hour of downtime. A character can spend one or more Hit Dice at the end of the rest to regain hit points.

Long Rest

At least 8 hours with sleep or light activity. A character regains all hit points and up to half their maximum number of Hit Dice.

Hit Dice

For each Hit Die spent, roll the die, add Constitution modifier, and regain that many hit points.

Exhaustion

A long rest can reduce exhaustion by 1 if the creature has food and drink. Some features or table rules may adjust this.
