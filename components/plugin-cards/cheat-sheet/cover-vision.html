Cover & Vision

Cover

Half cover +2 bonus to AC and Dexterity saving throws.
Three-quarters cover +5 bonus to AC and Dexterity saving throws.
Total cover Cannot be targeted directly by attacks or spells.

Obscured Areas

  • Lightly obscured: disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight.
  • Heavily obscured: creatures effectively suffer the blinded condition when trying to see into it.

Vision

Darkvision lets a creature see dim light as bright light and darkness as dim light within its range, but only in shades of gray.

Invisible

Attacks against an invisible creature have disadvantage, and the invisible creature's attacks have advantage unless another rule cancels it.
