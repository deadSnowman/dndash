Concentration

Checks

When a concentrating caster takes damage, make a Constitution saving throw. DC is 10 or half the damage taken, whichever is higher.

What Breaks It

  • Casting another spell that requires concentration.
  • Being incapacitated or killed.
  • Choosing to end concentration at any time.

Reminders

A caster can move, attack, and cast non-concentration spells while concentrating. Each separate damage source can trigger its own save.
