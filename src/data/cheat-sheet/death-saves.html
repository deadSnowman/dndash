Death Saves

At 0 HP

A creature usually falls unconscious. If remaining damage equals or exceeds its hit point maximum, it dies outright.

Death Saves

  • Roll a d20 at the start of each turn while dying.
  • 10 or higher: one success. 9 or lower: one failure.
  • Three successes: stable. Three failures: dead.
  • Natural 1: two failures. Natural 20: regain 1 hit point.

Damage

Taking damage while dying causes a failed death save. A critical hit causes two failures. Melee attacks from within 5 feet usually crit if they hit an unconscious target.

Stabilizing

A DC 10 Wisdom (Medicine) check can stabilize a creature. A stable creature remains at 0 HP but stops making death saves.
