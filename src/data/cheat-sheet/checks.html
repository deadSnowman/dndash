Social & Exploration Checks

Common Checks

InsightRead motives, lies, fear, or intent.
InvestigationFind clues, infer meaning, search details.
PerceptionNotice sights, sounds, tracks, or danger.
PersuasionGood-faith influence.
DeceptionLies, disguise, or misdirection.
IntimidationThreats, pressure, or fear.

Contests

Use opposed checks when two sides directly compete, such as Stealth vs Perception or Athletics vs Athletics.

Passive Checks

Passive score = 10 + modifiers. Add 5 for advantage and subtract 5 for disadvantage.
