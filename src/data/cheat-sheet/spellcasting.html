Spellcasting Basics

Attack & DC

  • Spell save DC = 8 + proficiency bonus + spellcasting ability modifier.
  • Spell attack modifier = proficiency bonus + spellcasting ability modifier.

Components

Verbal needs speech. Somatic needs a free hand. Material needs listed components or a focus unless a cost or consumption is specified.

Bonus Action Rule

If you cast a spell as a bonus action, the only other spell you can cast that turn is a cantrip with a casting time of 1 action.

Rituals

Ritual casting adds 10 minutes and does not spend a spell slot, if the caster has the ritual feature and the spell has the ritual tag.
