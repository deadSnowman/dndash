Objects & Environment

Object AC

Cloth, paper, ropeAC 11
Crystal, glass, iceAC 13
Wood, boneAC 15
StoneAC 17
Iron, steelAC 19

Object HP

Fragile objects might have 2 to 5 HP. Resilient objects might have 10 to 27 HP depending on size.

Improvised Damage

Use 1d10 for a minor hazard, 4d10 for a dangerous hazard, 10d10 for a deadly hazard, and 18d10 or more for extreme threats.

Hazards

For improvised saves, DC 10 is easy, DC 15 is moderate, and DC 20 is hard.
